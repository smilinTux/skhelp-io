🚀 Deploy Your AI Workforce

SKHelp is powered by SKCapstone — the sovereign agent framework. Deploy on your own server in minutes.

# Install SKCapstone (includes SKHelp)
pip install skcapstone

# Initialize SKHelp for your industry
skhelp init --industry real-estate --name "Acme Realty AI"

# Deploy your AI workforce
skhelp deploy --channels web,sms --auth capauth

# Monitor your agents
skhelp status --dashboard
